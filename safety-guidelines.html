Safety Guidelines

At SkillSwap, your safety is our priority. Follow these guidelines to ensure a secure and enjoyable experience while using our platform.

1. Verify User Profiles

Always check user profiles for verification badges and read reviews from other users. This helps ensure you are connecting with genuine individuals.

2. Communicate Safely

Use the platform's messaging system to communicate with other users. Avoid sharing personal contact information until you feel comfortable.

3. Meet in Public Places

If you are meeting someone in person, choose a public place for your first meeting. Inform a friend or family member about your plans.

4. Trust Your Instincts

If something feels off or uncomfortable, trust your instincts. You can always end a conversation or meeting if you feel uneasy.

5. Report Suspicious Activity

If you encounter any suspicious behavior or feel threatened, report it to our support team immediately. Your safety is our top concern.
