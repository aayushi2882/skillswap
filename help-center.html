Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is SkillSwap?
SkillSwap is a peer-to-peer skill exchange platform where you can teach and learn various skills from other users.
How do I create an account?
Click the Sign Up button on the homepage and fill out your information to get started.
How do I find skills on the platform?
Use the Skills section to browse trending skills or use filters to find exactly what you're interested in.
How do I connect with other users?
Send connection requests through skill posts and start chatting to arrange your sessions.
What is the payment process like?
SkillSwap is a free platform focused on skill exchange; no payments are involved.
How do I report suspicious activity?
Use the Report Issue link under Support or contact our help center immediately if you feel unsafe.
What are the community guidelines?
Please visit our Community Rules page for detailed guidelines to maintain a respectful community.
How do I troubleshoot common issues?
Many common solutions are available in this Help Center or contact support for assistance.
